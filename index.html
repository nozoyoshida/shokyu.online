System

Monthly flow

①Check monthly homework

Let's challenge the homework once a month. We carefully select the word that is beautiful, useful to know, and easy for beginners to work on. You can learn not only Shodo but also Japanese culture.

②Watch instruction videos

The instruction videos explain how to move the brush, the shape of the character, and useful tips to write well. The audio language is Japanese, but it has informative and easy-to-understand English subtitles.

③Practice

Let's actually challenge Shodo while referring to the model and videos. The key to writing well is to carefully observe the characteristics of the model characters (stroke length, margin balance, etc.). Ask 橘芳 -Kitsuho- if you have any questions; She will kindly support you.

④Scan & Send

After practicing, scan your works with your smartphone camera and send the image to 橘芳 -Kitsuho-. Since she writes the correction directly on the image of your work, it is easy to intuitively understand where to correct or where it is superior. Adobe scan is recommended as a scanning app (it's free).

⑤1 on 1 instruction

橘芳 -Kitsuho- will teach you 1 on 1 with image correction and detailed advice in text. Don't worry if you can't write well at first; Sensei has a lot of teaching experience and knows the problematic points for beginners. Since Sensei are not good at English, the support staff Nozo will support you as an interpreter.

⑥Practice again

The key to improving your calligraphy skills is to practice repeatedly. Let's try again by referring to the 1on1 instruction feedback, model, and instruction video. We guarantee that you will be able to write more beautifully than last time. Repeat 1-5 cycles once a week as a guide.
